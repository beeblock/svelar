## Interview Guide

### Introduction (5 min)
- Thank you for your time
- Purpose: [Brief explanation]
- Duration: [X minutes]
- Recording consent: "Is it okay if I record this for my notes?"
- Confidentiality: "Your responses will be kept confidential"
- No wrong answers: "We're testing the product, not you"

### Warm-up (5-10 min)
1. Tell me about your role and what you do day-to-day.
2. How long have you been in this role?
3. [Context-setting question]

### Context & Background (10 min)
1. Walk me through how you currently [do task related to research topic].
2. How often do you [do this task]?
3. What tools do you use?
4. Who else is involved?

### Deep Dive (25-30 min)
1. Tell me about the last time you [specific scenario].
   - What were you trying to accomplish?
   - What steps did you take?
   - What went well?
   - What was frustrating?

2. [Follow-up questions based on their response]
   - Why did you [do that]?
   - How did you feel when [that happened]?
   - What did you expect to happen?

3. [Repeat for other scenarios]

### Product-Specific (If Applicable) (10 min)
1. [Show prototype/product]
2. What do you think this is for?
3. How would you use this?
4. What questions do you have?
5. What would make this more useful?

### Wrap-up (5 min)
1. Is there anything else you think I should know?
2. If you had a magic wand, what would you change about [topic]?
3. Can we follow up if we have more questions?

**Total Duration**: 60 minutes

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Can't recruit enough participants | Start early, use multiple channels, increase incentive |
| Participants don't show up | Over-recruit by 20%, send reminders |
| Technical issues with recording | Test equipment, have backup method |
| Bias in questions | Pilot test guide, use open-ended questions |
